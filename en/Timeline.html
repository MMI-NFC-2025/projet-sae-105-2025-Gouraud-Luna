Henrietta Leavitt Astronomer
Discovered the relation between the brightness and the Cepheides phase (dimensions of distances in the’univers)
It’s Hubble who obtain all the glory of this discovery
1868-1921

Lise Meitner Nuclear Physicist
Discovered nuclear fusion with Otto Hahn
Otto Hahn received the Nobel Prize, alone
1878-1968

Nettie Stevens Geneticist
Discovered the role of chromosomes X and Y determining the gender of a human
Its Hunt Morgan who received all of the credit
1861-1912

Rosalind Franklin Physical Chemestist
She discovered the double helix structure of the ADN
But it’s Watson, Crick and Wilkins who have been awarded the Nobel Prize of Medicine
1920-1958

Esther Lederberg Microbiologist
Discovered the bacterial culture by replication
It's her husband who obtained the Nobel Prize without her.
1922-2006
