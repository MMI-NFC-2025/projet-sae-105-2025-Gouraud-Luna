Project presentation

This site was created as part of an educational project in the first year of MMi.
The objective here is to educate people to the Matilda effect through a scientist, Jocelyn Bell Burnell, to shed light on the contribution of women in science history, and thus encouraging a more equitable recognition between men and women.
